UML-to-Code Activity
Payroll Application - Problem Statement
You have been hired as part of a global team put together to develop a Payroll Application for a
new and fast-growing company located in the Western Cape, with headquarters in Cape Town,
called STARTUP. STARTUP now has 2000 employees, and it has become very difficult to manage
their payroll system manually. The chief technical officer (CTO) feels that is about time they
automated their payroll system to provide better information to management about their
Human Resources.
The payroll system should manage employees. Each employee has a mandatory employee
number, a name, employment type (permanent or contract) and nationality. Employee also has
contact details and address details. The contact details comprise of a cell phone number, email
and home number, while the address should include street address and postal address.
The company also keeps track of all the types of identities and values that an employee wishes
to use. For example, the employee can have a type of identity called Passport with value of
“ZG1234”.
The STARTUP also keeps track of jobs identified by their titles. Each job can have several
positions uniquely identified with a position code. An employee can only fill one position at a
time. If the position is filled, the status of the position is changed to CLOSED, otherwise it is left
as OPEN.
To speed up the development, the project has been divided into three layers: the Presentation
layer, the Service layer and the data storage layer called the Repository. The presentation layer
has been given to the other team members.
